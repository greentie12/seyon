MSEF Judges Feedback for the Smart Recycling Project

Hi Seyon Wijendran,

Below we have included feedback provided by the judges that reviewed your entry "The Future of Smart Recycling" . We hope you find this feedback useful.

Judge 1

Feedback:

 Very nicely done project. You did a lot of work in figuring out how to improve the accuracy of the system.

Judge 2

Feedback:

 You might have a commercial product here.

Judge 3

Feedback:

 original concept and design with future applications possible! well done

Judge 4

Feedback:

 Very interesting project and has real life potential. Presentation was well done. Very interesting use of AI to very real problem in our communities. Continue the interest and hard work.

Judge 5

Feedback:

 Please present this project to your municipal environmental management/EPA. The possible extensions to your project are intriguing. In your presentation, include the sources for facts you present. Your diligence in formulate-test-analyze-fix sequence is admirable. This is a product you should be very proud of.

Judge 6

Feedback:

 These two students did an excellent job of designing a great tool for identifying recyclable plastic. They were able to answer all of our questions and I wish them great luck in the next phase. I liked how they overcame some programming and software challenges and devised adequate solutions.

Thank you.
