Community Service

I enjoy volunteering actively to make a positive impact in my community. Partnering with MA-based non-profits such as Immigrant Family Services Institute (IFSI-USA), I have implemented and taught coding (Python) to youth during summer. I tutor math and English throughout the academic year. I help with fundraising for non-profits such as Greater Boston Food Bank and Manitha Neyam Trust, playing piano and raising awareness. As a leader of my high school community club, I actively participate in volunteer activities to benefit my school and town, including town-cleanups and book drives.

Volunteer Academic Teaching and Tutoring

I, along with a teammate, developed curriculum, and started a virtual Python coding summer enrichment program at IFSI-USA. I have also taught robotics skills at IFSI.
